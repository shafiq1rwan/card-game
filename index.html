ⓘ How to Play

  • Pick your favourite card. Each card shows HP (top-left), the dice it needs for ⚔️ ATK / 🛡️ DEF and its ↻ reroll chances (bottom), plus a ✨ special effect.
  • A coin flip decides who attacks first — attacker and defender swap every round.
  • On your turn, roll 5 dice, then tap dice to select exactly the number your card needs. Their sum becomes your ATK or DEF.
  • Selected dice can be rerolled instead — each card has 2 or 3 reroll chances per turn.
  • Meet your card's ✨ special condition with the kept dice for bonus power.
  • Damage = ATK − DEF. Bring the enemy's HP to 0 to win!
  • 🔥 Sudden Death: from Round 7 onward, every attack gains +1 ATK per extra round — long duels always reach a finish.
