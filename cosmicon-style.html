ⓘ How to Play

  • Pick your favourite card. Each card has HP (top-left), required dice for ⚔️ ATK and 🛡️ DEF (bottom hexes), reroll chances (↻), and a ✨ special effect.
  • A coin flip decides who attacks first. Attacker and defender swap every turn.
  • On your turn you throw 5 dice, then select exactly the number your card requires and Confirm — their sum is your ATK or DEF value.
  • You may reroll dice you select — each card has 2 or 3 reroll chances per turn.
  • Meet your card's ✨ special condition with the dice you keep to gain bonus ATK/DEF.
  • Damage = ATK − DEF. Reduce the enemy's HP to 0 to win the duel!
  • 🔥 Sudden Death: from Round 7 onward every attack gains +1 ATK per extra round, so long duels always reach a finish.
